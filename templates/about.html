About PoultryDetect

PoultryDetect is a smart AI-powered web application that uses deep learning to help poultry farmers and veterinarians detect diseases in chickens through image analysis.

  • Mission: Empower poultry owners with fast, reliable, and accessible disease detection using technology.
  • How it Works: Users upload an image of a chicken. The AI model classifies whether the bird is healthy or suffering from diseases such as Coccidiosis, Salmonella, or Newcastle Disease.
  • Technologies Used: Python, Flask, TensorFlow/Keras, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS, JavaScript.
  • Benefits: Early disease detection, cost-effective screening, and reduced poultry mortality.

Whether you are a poultry farmer, student, or developer, PoultryDetect aims to demonstrate the practical use of AI in agriculture and animal health.
